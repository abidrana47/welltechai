Overview

Support between clinical touchpoints

Our recovery support AI provides structured check-ins, coping strategy tools, and crisis-aware escalation — built to reinforce therapy plans and maintain engagement across the full recovery journey, not just during appointments.

  • Daily check-ins with adaptive recovery prompts and mood tracking
  • Relapse prevention pathways and real-time risk monitoring
  • Trauma-informed dialogue aligned to psychological safety principles
  • Clinical escalation for safeguarding, triage, and crisis response
  • Outcome reporting for care teams and commissioners
  • Personalised coping plans updated through AI-driven conversation

Key Outcomes

Designed for safer, more consistent recovery engagement at every stage of the pathway.

  • Reduced gaps between sessions and clinical appointments
  • Increased adherence to personalised recovery plans
  • Early risk alerts enabling timely clinical intervention
  • Measurable improvement in patient-reported wellbeing scores
Features

Built for safe, effective recovery support

Every feature designed around the clinical, safeguarding, and psychological complexity of addiction recovery.

Trauma-Informed Dialogue

Conversation flows designed with clinical psychologists — using language that builds trust, maintains psychological safety, and adapts sensitively to a patient's emotional state and recovery stage.

24/7 Always-On Engagement

Recovery doesn't pause between appointments. Our AI is available around the clock — delivering consistent, structured support at the moments patients are most vulnerable to relapse.

Relapse Risk Monitoring

Continuous analysis of conversation signals, mood patterns, and self-reported data to detect early indicators of relapse risk — with automated escalation to clinical teams when thresholds are reached.

Personalised Recovery Plans

Each patient receives a tailored recovery pathway — with adaptive coping strategies, milestone celebrations, and daily prompts shaped by their individual goals, triggers, and progress history.

Clinical Escalation Pathways

Configurable crisis detection with direct escalation to keyworkers, clinical teams, or emergency services — ensuring safeguarding obligations are met without burdening frontline staff.

Outcome & Compliance Reporting

Commissioners and clinical leads gain real-time visibility into engagement rates, recovery milestones, and risk trends — with exportable reports aligned to NHS and local authority frameworks.

Data Sovereignty

Security & Compliance by Design

Architected around UK regulatory requirements and clinical safety standards from day one.

Enterprise-Grade Data Sovereignty

Every WellTechAI CIC system is built security-first. Patient data never leaves UK shores protected by clinical-grade encryption and UK governance frameworks.

UK GDPR Compliance

Fully aligned with the UK General Data Protection Regulation and Data Protection Act 2018. All health data processing documented under Article 30 with lawful basis for special category data under Article 9.

End-to-End Encryption

AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Zero-knowledge architecture for sensitive health records no plaintext patient data at any system layer.

UK-Based Data Residency

Patient data never leaves UK jurisdiction. Hosted exclusively on UK-region cloud infrastructure with no cross-border transfers guaranteed by contractual and technical controls.

Clinical Safety (DCB0129 Principles)

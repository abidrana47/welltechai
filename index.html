Data Sovereignty

Security & Compliance by Design

Architected around UK regulatory requirements and clinical safety standards from the very first line of code.

🔐 Enterprise-Grade Data Sovereignty

Every WellTechAI system is built security-first. Patient data never leaves UK shores — every touchpoint is protected by clinical-grade encryption and governance frameworks.

🇬🇧

UK GDPR Compliance

Fully aligned with the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018. All health data processing is documented under Article 30 with lawful basis for special category data under Article 9.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Zero-knowledge architecture for sensitive health records. No plaintext patient data at any system layer.

🏥

UK-Based Data Residency

Patient and clinical data never leaves UK jurisdiction. Hosted exclusively on UK-region cloud infrastructure with no cross-border data transfers — guaranteed by contractual and technical controls.

⚕️

Clinical Safety Protocols (DCB0129)

Software developed under NHS Digital's DCB0129 clinical risk management standard. A qualified Clinical Safety Officer oversees all product releases entering any UK health pathway.

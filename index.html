Data Sovereignty and Compliance

Built for the Highest Standard of Clinical Safety

Healthcare data demands more than industry-standard security. Our infrastructure is designed for the UK's strictest regulatory and ethical obligations.

🛡
UK GDPR Compliant

Full compliance with UK GDPR and ICO guidance for health data processing. Data subject rights enforced by design, not afterthought.

🔐
End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted and stored using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. Zero unencrypted data paths across our entire stack.

🇬🇧
UK-Based Data Residency

All patient and health data processed and stored exclusively within the United Kingdom. No cross-border transfers. Sovereign infrastructure only.

⚕
DCB0129 Clinical Safety

Adherence to NHS Digital's DCB0129 clinical risk management standard. Full Clinical Safety Case documentation for every product we deploy.

🔏

Data Sovereignty Statement

WellTechAI operates a strict data sovereignty policy. We will never process, transfer, or share identifiable health data outside UK jurisdiction. Our systems undergo regular third-party penetration testing and clinical safety review. We do not use patient data to train external AI models.

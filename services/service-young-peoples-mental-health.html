Overview

What this service does

This service supports young people with anxiety, stress, self-esteem, and social pressures through accessible, youth-friendly digital support. It helps users build coping skills, stay engaged with practical self-help, and reach the right level of support more quickly when challenges escalate.

Ideal For

Designed for youth services, colleges, charities, and community mental health pathways supporting ages 16 to 25.

  • Colleges and sixth form wellbeing teams
  • Youth hubs and community support services
  • Charities supporting young people's wellbeing
  • Digital mental health pathways for ages 16 to 25

Compliance & Data Protection

Built for governed, privacy-first digital care

Every WellTechAI CIC service is designed around privacy, clinical governance, accessibility, and safeguarding so organisations can deploy with confidence.

🔒 GDPR Compliant

All services fully comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the EU GDPR. User data is processed lawfully, transparently, and for specified purposes only.

🔒 Fully Licensed

All AI health bots operate under appropriate healthcare licensing frameworks. Clinical content is reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals.

🔒 Data Encryption

All conversations are encrypted end-to-end. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

🔒 Safeguarding

Robust safeguarding protocols are embedded in every bot. Crisis situations are detected and escalated immediately to appropriate emergency services.

🔒 Clinical Governance

All health information is evidence-based, regularly reviewed, and aligned with NICE guidelines, CDC recommendations, WHO best practice, and CQC-aligned governance expectations.

🔒 Right to Erasure

Users can request complete deletion of their data at any time, in accordance with their rights under GDPR Article 17.

🔒 Accessibility

All services meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards and are available in multiple languages.

Overview

Personalised guidance that sticks

Our weight management bot blends nutrition science with evidence-based behavioural coaching. It helps patients build sustainable habits through personalised meal planning, activity nudges, and progress insights while giving clinicians and commissioners the data they need to evidence outcomes.

  • Tailored meal and activity plans based on individual goals and conditions
  • Adaptive coaching and daily motivational check-ins
  • Integration with wearables, Apple Health, and Google Fit
  • Food logging with nutrient analysis aligned to NICE guidance
  • Outcome tracking for population health and commissioner reporting
  • Clinical escalation when weight or health indicators cross thresholds

Key Outcomes

Built for measurable results across NHS weight management pathways and public health programmes.

  • Higher programme adherence through consistent daily nudges
  • Improved engagement across 8 to 16 week structured plans
  • Clinical dashboards for ongoing review and escalation
  • Population-level reporting aligned to NHS outcome frameworks
Features

Built for lasting behaviour change

Every feature grounded in NICE guidelines, behavioural science, and the practical realities of NHS weight management delivery.

Adaptive Daily Coaching

AI-powered check-ins that adapt to each patient's progress, mood, and reported barriers delivering motivational messaging rooted in CBT and behaviour change theory, every single day.

NICE-Aligned Nutrition Guidance

Food logging with intelligent nutrient analysis, portion guidance, and meal recommendations all aligned to NICE weight management guidelines and tailored to patient comorbidities and dietary needs.

Wearable & App Integration

Real-time sync with Fitbit, Apple Health, Google Fit, and other wearable platforms automatically incorporating step counts, activity data, and sleep patterns into personalised coaching responses.

Personalised Goal Tracking

Structured 8 to 16 week programmes with milestone tracking, BMI and weight trend visualisation, and adaptive plan updates keeping patients motivated and clinicians informed throughout.

Clinical Threshold Alerts

Automated flagging when patient weight, reported symptoms, or health indicators cross clinical thresholds enabling timely intervention without placing additional burden on frontline teams.

Commissioner Outcome Dashboards

Population-level analytics covering programme adherence, weight loss trajectories, and engagement trends in reporting formats aligned to NHS and local authority outcome frameworks.

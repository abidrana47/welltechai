01

Overview

This Cookie Policy applies to www.welltechai.co.uk and explains our use of cookies and similar technologies.

We use strictly necessary cookies to keep the website working and to remember your cookie preferences. We only use optional analytics cookies if you choose to allow them.

Choice and Control

You can accept all cookies, reject optional cookies, or choose your preferences. You can also change your choices at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

02

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help a site function correctly, remember your choices, and provide usage insights.

  • First-party cookies are set by this website.
  • Third-party cookies are set by external services we use (for example analytics providers).
  • Session cookies expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies remain for a defined period unless deleted sooner.
03

Cookies We Use

CookieTypePurposeTypical duration
welltech_cookie_consent Necessary Stores your cookie preferences so the site can remember your choice. 90 days
_ga Optional analytics Distinguishes users for aggregate website analytics reporting. Up to 2 years
_gid Optional analytics Distinguishes users for short-term analytics measurement. Up to 24 hours
_gat (if used) Optional analytics Helps throttle analytics request rates. Up to 1 minute

Optional analytics cookies are not set unless you actively allow them.

04

Legal Basis and UK Rules

In the UK, cookies are governed mainly by the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), with UK GDPR applying to personal data collected through those cookies.

  • Necessary cookies: used because they are essential for the website to function and to remember your preferences.
  • Optional analytics cookies: only used when you provide consent through the cookie banner/settings.
  • You can withdraw or change consent at any time without losing access to core website functionality.
05

Third-Party Cookies

If optional analytics is enabled, analytics technology may place third-party cookies on your device. Those providers may process data under their own terms and privacy notices.

Important

We configure analytics only after consent. If you reject optional cookies, analytics cookies remain disabled.

06

Managing Your Cookie Choices

  • On your first visit, you can accept all, reject optional, or open cookie settings.
  • You can reopen settings any time from the Cookie Settings link in the website footer.
  • You can also manage cookies through your browser settings and clear cookies from your device.

Open Cookie Settings now

07

Cookie Duration and Retention

Cookie expiry depends on the cookie type. Preference cookies may persist for set periods (for example 90 days), while some analytics cookies may expire sooner.

If you clear cookies in your browser, your previous preferences may be removed and you may see the cookie banner again on your next visit.

08

Contact and Policy Updates

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, email info@welltechai.co.uk.

If you are unhappy with how cookies are handled, you can raise concerns with the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

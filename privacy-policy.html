01

Introduction

WellTechAI CIC ("we", "us", "our") operates www.welltechai.co.uk. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our behavioral health AI services.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our website you agree to the practices described here.

Our Compliance Commitment

We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and NHS Digital's DCB0129 clinical safety standard.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, email address and phone number
  • Organisation name and role (for NHS partners)
  • Messages submitted via contact forms
  • Health and wellness data — only with your explicit consent through our behavioral health applications

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system and browser
  • IP address and approximate location (country/city level)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral source and on-site interactions

Third-Party Sources

  • NHS Digital systems (when our AI applications are deployed in NHS settings)
  • Publicly available business registries and NHS directories
04

How We Use Your Information

  • Delivering our behavioral health AI solutions and responding to pilot program requests
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending service updates and, with your consent, newsletters
  • Analysing website usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Research and development on our AI health applications
  • Complying with healthcare regulations, NHS requirements and UK law
  • Protecting against fraud and maintaining audit trails for clinical governance
  • Sending marketing communications about relevant health technology with your consent only
05

Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR Article 6)

Consent Article 6(1)(a)

Marketing communications and health data in behavioral health applications.

Contract Performance Article 6(1)(b)

Delivering our services and fulfilling pilot programs for NHS trusts.

Legal Obligation Article 6(1)(c)

Compliance with healthcare regulations, NHS requirements and UK law.

Legitimate Interests Article 6(1)(f)

Improving our services and website analytics. Health data is never processed under this basis.

06

Health & Special Category Data (GDPR Article 9)

Important

Health data (smoking habits, weight, addiction history) is special category data under GDPR Article 9. We only process it with your explicit consent, for provision of requested health services, or where required by law.

  • All health data is encrypted end-to-end (AES-256)
  • Stored in UK data centres only no cross-border transfers
  • Access restricted to authorised healthcare professionals and AI systems
  • Full audit trails maintained for all data access
07

Who We Share Your Data With

We Do Not Sell Your Data

WellTechAI does not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party providers necessary to deliver our services, including cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure), analytics (Google Analytics), email services and payment processors. All are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) requiring GDPR compliance.

NHS & Health Organisations

When you use our solutions within an NHS setting, we share necessary data with the relevant healthcare professionals, clinical safety teams and data governance staff.

Legal & Regulatory Authorities

We may disclose data when required by court order, regulatory request (ICO, NHS England) or to protect our legal rights and prevent fraud.

Business Transfers

If WellTechAI is acquired or merged, your data may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

Data TypeRetention Period

10

Data Security

  • End-to-end AES-256 encryption for data at rest; TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Zero-knowledge architecture health data is never accessible in plaintext
  • All patient data hosted exclusively in UK data centres
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication for all staff
  • Full audit trails for every data access event
  • Annual security audits and penetration testing
  • ISO 27001 information security management and DCB0129 clinical safety compliance
Security Limitation

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at info@welltechai.co.uk.

11

Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15–21)

All requests are responded to within 30 days (up to 90 days for complex cases). To exercise any right, email info@welltechai.co.uk with your name, a description of your request and proof of identity.

Article 15

Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Article 16

Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Article 17

Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

Article 18

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data while a request is reviewed.

Article 20

Portability

Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

Article 21

Object

Object to processing for marketing or legitimate interest purposes.

12

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware a child has provided data, we will delete it immediately and inform the parent or guardian.

For behavioral health services involving minors, parental consent is required in line with NHS safeguarding policies.

Overview

Always-on support that scales clinical reach

Our smoking cessation AI agents deliver personalised, evidence-based guidance 24 hours a day — filling critical gaps between clinical appointments with consistent, structured engagement rooted in CBT and motivational interviewing frameworks.

  • Real-time adaptive conversations using CBT and MI techniques
  • Personalised quit plans with milestone tracking and nudges
  • Craving and trigger management with coping strategy prompts
  • Clinical escalation pathways and safeguarding protocols
  • Outcome reporting for care teams and commissioners

Key Outcomes

Designed to drive measurable quit rates and sustained engagement at population scale.

  • Up to 3× increase in quit attempt completion rates

  • Real-time risk signals for timely clinical intervention
  • Cost-effective support across large patient populations
Features

Built for clinical effectiveness

Every feature is informed by evidence-based cessation frameworks and NHS digital standards.

NLP-Powered Dialogue

Advanced natural language processing enables nuanced, human-feeling conversations that adapt to tone, readiness to quit, and emotional state in real time.

24/7 Always-On Engagement

Cravings don't keep office hours. Our agents are available around the clock — delivering support exactly when patients need it most, without clinical overhead.

CBT & Motivational Interviewing

Conversations are structured around proven cessation psychology — identifying triggers, challenging automatic thoughts, and building intrinsic motivation for lasting change.

Personalised Quit Planning

Each patient receives a tailored quit journey — from setting a quit date to tracking milestones — with adaptive nudges based on behavioural signals and self-reported progress.

Risk Monitoring & Escalation

Automatic detection of distress signals, relapse risk, or safeguarding concerns — with configurable escalation pathways directly to clinical teams or crisis services.

Outcome Analytics Dashboard

Commissioners and clinicians gain full visibility into quit rates, engagement trends, and population-level health data — in compliant, exportable reporting formats.
